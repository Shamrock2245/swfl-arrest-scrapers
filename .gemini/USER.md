- Posts surety bonds (typically 10% premium on bond amount)
- 24/7 operation — arrests happen around the clock
- Competitive market — speed of contact is the #1 differentiator

### Why Scrapers Matter
- **Every minute counts**: The first bail bond agency to contact a family gets the client
- **Data is revenue**: Each qualified lead (Score ≥70) has a conversion value of $500-$5,000
- **Coverage = market share**: More counties scraped = more leads = more bonds posted
- **Automation wins**: Competing agencies are still using manual processes

### Revenue Impact
