HCSO Arrest Inquiry

Important Notice

This database lists people currently in jail and includes information on their charges, bond amount, and booking photo. An arrest does not mean that the inmate has been convicted of a crime.

This information is updated every 30 minutes. When a person’s information is entered into the Hillsborough County Sheriff’s Office Jail Management System, that information is updated to this listing within 30 minutes. A person will appear in these listings for 90 days after their release date (unless you are an authorized member).

For information on how to get an arrest record sealed or expunged (removed), please visit the Florida Department of Law Enforcement website at https://www.fdle.state.fl.us/seal-and-expunge-process/seal-and-expunge-home.aspx.

For statewide criminal history, information, please contact the Florida Department of Law Enforcement, Public Records, (850) 410-8109 or visit their website at: https://web.fdle.state.fl.us/search/app/default.

Information provided should not be relied upon for any type of legal action.

Arrest Inquiry Full Access

The ONLY individuals or entities that are allowed full access to the portal are Law Enforcement Agencies and select individuals associated with the bond industry, accredited media or any recognized State Bar association. Please do not request access to the system unless you meet the above criteria. Individuals who qualify should forward an email through their official email account requesting access to the system to publicaffairs@teamhcso.com. Final approval of all requests remain with HCSO.

Upon receiving approval, applicants will receive an email letting them know they can begin the registration process.

  Important Notice:
